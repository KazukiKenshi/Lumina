Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)
----------------------------------
Characterized by excessive and uncontrollable worry about various events or activities,
occurring more days than not for at least 6 months.

Symptoms:
- Restlessness or feeling on edge
- Fatigue
- Difficulty concentrating
- Irritability
- Muscle tension
- Sleep disturbances

The anxiety is difficult to control and causes distress or impairment.
