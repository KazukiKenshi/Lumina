Digital Hygiene & Mental Space
------------------------------
Common Strains:
- Endless scrolling fragments attention.
- Late-night blue light delays sleep onset.
- Constant notifications elevate baseline stress.

Core Practices:
1. Notification audit: Disable non-essential push alerts.
2. App zoning: Place high-distraction apps on a secondary screen/folder.
3. Time windows: Define intentional check-in blocks (e.g., messages at :00 and :30 marks).
4. Screen curfew: 45+ minutes pre-sleep transition to low-stimulation tasks.
5. Single-task mode: Fullscreen windows; hide other tabs while focused.
